Frequently Asked Questions

What types of businesses is OptimaFlow best suited for?

OptimaFlow is designed to help businesses of all sizes streamline their operations. We serve startups, SMBs, and enterprise clients across various industries including tech, finance, healthcare, e-commerce, and professional services. Our platform is particularly valuable for companies with repetitive workflows that can benefit from automation.

How long does it take to set up?

Most customers can get started with basic workflows in under an hour. The time to implement more complex automations depends on your specific needs, but our intuitive interface and pre-built templates help accelerate the process. We also offer onboarding assistance for all paid plans.

What integrations do you support?

OptimaFlow integrates with over 500 popular business applications including Salesforce, Slack, Google Workspace, Microsoft 365, QuickBooks, Shopify, and many more. We also provide API access for custom integrations. Our integration library is constantly expanding based on customer needs.

Is my data secure with OptimaFlow?

Security is our top priority. We use enterprise-grade encryption (AES-256) for data at rest and in transit, comply with SOC 2 Type II standards, and undergo regular third-party security audits. You maintain ownership of all your data, and we never sell or share it with third parties.

Can I change plans or cancel anytime?

Yes! You can upgrade, downgrade, or cancel your subscription at any time through your account settings. We don't lock you into long-term contracts, though we do offer discounts for annual billing if you prefer.
